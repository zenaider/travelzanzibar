The Best Beaches

Tanzania, located on the eastern coast of Africa, is renowned for its stunning beaches along the Indian Ocean. Here are a few popular Tanzanian beach destinations: Zanzibar: Zanzibar Island, a semi-autonomous region of Tanzania, boasts some of the most beautiful beaches in the country. The island is famous for its pristine white sandy shores, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant coral reefs. Popular beaches in Zanzibar include Nungwi, Kendwa, Paje, and Matemwe.
